Crystals Collector

How To Play

You are given a randm target number. Each crystal is assigned a random value, which is hidden from you.

Your job is to click on the crystals to get your score to match the target number. Your score will go up each time you click on a crystal. Pay attention to how much each crystal is worth as you click on them.

If you can match your score to the target number, you win! If you go over the target number, you lose. After each round, a new random target number will be selected and the crystals will be assigned new random values.
